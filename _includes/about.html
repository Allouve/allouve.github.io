About

The Purpose Built Group LLC, doing business as Allouve, is a black-owned, limited liability corporation, originally founded in 2015, with a mission to serve purpose-driven organizations that are working to better humanity. Allouve provides strategic and technical services and support to public, non-profit, and private sector companies focusing on equity.

Brandon Rapp

Brandon Rapp is a seasoned consultant and project manager with extensive experience in leading data systems projects for public, private, and non-profit clients. His expertise lies in driving the strategic development and operational execution of data infrastructure initiatives. Brandon's current data advisory role with a national non-profit showcases his ability to manage and evaluate complex data migrations and implement enterprise-level analytics solutions. Prior to Allouve, Brandon worked with WIOA and youth programs as a strategic consultant and advisor and was a former high school educator.
